## Scope

Deliberately bounded to **the two books read**. The rest of the Throne of Glass series, the ACOTAR series, and Crescent City are **left out by design** — to be catalogued when read. The persona `.agent`s are written strictly to book-one knowledge and then **adversarially checked to strip any later-book spoiler**.

## The ACI badge

Every ACI carries the full complement, in the format the standard specifies:

- **`.carbon` → TIFF** — the warm sigil, archival and lossless (`II*\0`)
- **`.silicon` → PNG** — the cool sigil, for the web
- plus `.agent` · `.spun` (the five W's) · `.moniker` (the tokenized mythos) · `.1099` (the credit ledger) · `manifest.dlw.json`

The main **MAAS** badge lives in [`maas.dlw/`](maas.dlw/).
